Our Villas

We are pleased to introduce our Forest Point Homes, it is just not a home stay. it's an experience, essence of nature living & creator of magic & memories. It’s just 5km from Bannerghatta Circle. This property is located on a cliff overlooking the Bannerghatta Forest. The doors would open to the valley beyond. It is built with a dream of living in sustainable homes, these mud row villas have a flourishing fruit orchard and eco-friendly ethnic architecture. This property is around 1.5 acres surrounded by a thick cover of large trees and lush green vegetation. The idea was to have three ethnic fusion villas that blend ancient looks from the outside with vernacular architecture on the inside.

We have a huge outdoor lawn to host small-to-mid size events & 3 mud houses with 12 bedrooms for accommodation & lots of space for parking
